Singapore Life!

Local

Lu Rou Fan (Braised pork rice) @ Seah Im Food Centre

This was one of my favourite lunches in Singapore. I would have it almost every day while I was on my 2-month internship near Harbourfront centre. The portions are very generous, the price is super affordable and the braised pork practically melts in your mouth! TIP: The queue would always be really long and the food runs out really quickly, so be sure to get there early!

Tourist

Historical market of Lau Pa Sat @ Telok Ayer market on the Raffles Quay

This amazing market dating back to the 19th century is for a me a key landmark of singapore. There you have many options but you should try the Hainanese Chicken Rice, Fish Ball Soup and the Wonton Soup but also the Dim Sum and Dumplings. TIP: ask the locals what they recommend always good way to experience Singapore from the inside.
